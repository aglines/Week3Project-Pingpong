Pingpong is a game of numbers.

Nota Bene:
  • Acceptable input* is a positive integer.
  • Multiples of 3, 5, and 15 are SPECIAL.
  • Entries with decimal places will be truncated to the nearest lower integer.
  • Negative numbers will be converted to positive numbers.

* "Acceptable input" might mean different things if you are testing software. See what you can get away with...
